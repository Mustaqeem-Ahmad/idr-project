About IDR

Built for the Hard
Edges of Modern Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment organisation. We exist to close the gap between what universities teach and what regulated industries actually need from digital, cyber, and technology risk professionals.

Our programmes are designed in direct partnership with UK universities and informed by practitioners operating at the frontier of financial services, critical national infrastructure, and AI-driven environments.

Students don't just study risk — they work on live projects alongside professionals and graduate deployment-ready. IDR is not a traditional training provider; we combine academic partnership, industry mentorship, and a structured deployment model.

Our Methodology

The IDR Deployment Pipeline

A four-stage model that takes individuals from structured learning through to active deployment in risk-critical roles.

01

Train

Rigorous, framework-grounded programmes delivered with university partners. Theory, simulation, and live case work.

02

Hire

Direct placement of IDR-assessed professionals with employers in financial services, CNI, and regulated tech environments.

03

Innovate

Deployed practitioners contribute to IDR research — building new risk models and governance tools that feed back into future programmes.

04

Deploy

Placement into live operational roles with continued IDR community access, CPD support, and an ongoing practitioner network.
